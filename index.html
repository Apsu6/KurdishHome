How to Order

How to Order

You can order any book by sending us a DM(Direct Message) on our social media accounts and the message should contain your book name or book cover , your full name ,your address and your phone number.
After sending us your information we will inform you about the price and once you confirm, it will be delivered to you.
The arrival time from abroad is approximately from 3 to 4 weeks.



About Us

About Us

We are an online-based bookstore established in 2017 in Sulaymaniah city in Kurdistan region of Iraq. At first we delivered only to Sulaymaniah city but as of now tens of thousands of books have found their homes on bookshelves all over Kurdistan and Iraq. We decided to create this online bookstore since there werent many original books available in the region. But now, readers can simply order their favorite original books from abroad with the click of a button and the books gets delivered to their doorsteps with an affordable price. We hope to see more and more readers in the future because books are truly essential even in our technological era, thus we dont want readers to be deprived of high quality books.
